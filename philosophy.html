Our Philosophy

The name “SterioshTech” is build around two words: Steriosh and Technology Steriosh is actually gotten from the Greek word “στερεός” which is spelt “stereos”. As an adjective, it means to be firm and stable in shape; not liquid or fluid; dependable; reliable, solid, steadfast, sturdy, blocky, durable, fast.

The word technology is our preference for solutions that are digital in nature and futuristic in impact, with a focus on sustainability and social-economic empowerment.

We believe our work should "develop Technology that bridges the gap".

Passion: Structured around people:

Our intend is to find passion, and invest in passion: each member is carefully selected with a mindset to explore the depth of his/her creativity. Working with Steriosh isn’t trading your soul; it is finding your soul.

Trust: The team interactions are built around trust:

I trust my teammates to deliver on tasks that they agree to. I also would ensure I deliver on my task without trying to hide behind their successes. This way, independently and together, we are stronger.

Family: We are a team, and then we are family:

We all want to be here and build the company cause it is ours. It belongs to us. We share in its glory and in its shame.

Empowerment: We build and invest in the people

Steriosh would train us, it would nurture us. It would take care of our needs, as we strive to take care of its name and accomplishments.
